About Serenity Grove

Compassionate care in a family-oriented environment

At Serenity Grove, we believe in providing exceptional care that honors the dignity and individuality of each resident. Our family-owned adult family home offers a warm, supportive environment where your loved ones can thrive.

With over 15 years of experience in senior care, our dedicated team is committed to creating a safe, comfortable, and engaging community for all our residents. We understand that choosing the right care for your loved one is one of the most important decisions you'll make, which is why we go above and beyond to create a nurturing environment that feels like home.

Our facility is specially designed to provide both comfort and safety, with spacious common areas, beautiful outdoor spaces, and private rooms that can be personalized to make each resident feel truly at home. We pride ourselves on our compassionate staff who are available 24/7 to provide personalized care and attention.
